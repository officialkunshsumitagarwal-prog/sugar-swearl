Our Story

Sugar Swirl is more than just a bakery. It is a story of passion, growth, and a shared dream between a mother and daughter.

It all began during the lockdown, when baking started as a simple interest for my mom.

What began in our home kitchen soon turned into something much more meaningful.

Her very first order came from within our own building, and from that moment on, there was no looking back.

Aneri Parikh (Cake artist)

The Beginning

With every cake, she learned, improved, and poured her heart into perfecting her craft.

A small beginning

At that time, I was in 10th grade, just helping her out, unaware that this small beginning would slowly become such an important part of my life too.

A shared journey

What started as lending a hand in the kitchen turned into a journey we now share together.

A different dream

I always imagined a future in interior design, creating spaces, working with aesthetics, and building something beautiful.

A sweeter path

But life had sweeter plans. Somewhere along the way, I discovered a new kind of art, one that could be created, felt, and tasted.

From Tasty Delight to Sugar Swirl

From designing spaces, I found myself designing cakes, and that is when I truly became a cake artist.

01

Our first name

Our journey began with the name Tasty Delight, a name that holds our first steps, our early orders, and countless learning experiences.

02

Why we changed

As we grew, we realized we were becoming something more. Our creations were no longer just tasty, they were thoughtful, detailed, and crafted with love.

03

A new chapter

At the end of 2024, we took a step forward and became Sugar Swirl, a name that truly reflects who we are today: a little more us, a little more creative, and a little more magical.

Today

Sugar Swirl stands as a dream built together, rooted in passion, creativity, and the joy of making people’s moments sweeter.

Every cake we create carries a piece of our journey, our learning, and our love. And honestly, it has been nothing short of magical.
